# RITE Research Skill

Use this skill before broad RITE adoption or schema hardening decisions.

## Research pass

1. Compare the proposed ritual/meaning model to at least three grounded
   references: anthropology, history of religion, ritual studies, legal/civic
   legitimacy, mourning practice, or game-system precedent.
2. Separate evidence-backed obligation from speculative game pressure.
3. Identify which downstream repo owns interpretation or mechanics.
4. Record citations in the relevant RITE wave or pulse doc before adoption.
