# RITE Phases

## Phase 1 - RITE Foundation

Establish the repo, Rust primitives, seed fixture, validation command, and
dependency placement for ritual and meaning-under-pressure records.

Exit criteria:

- `rite-core` and `rite-cli` validate the seed rite fixture.
- PRODUCT_PLAN names rite, obligation, taboo, sacred-place, calendar, and
  evidence boundaries.
- TRACKER records RITE as a Knowledge Systems submodule.
- Dependency trackers identify planned PROOF/CROP/PEBBLE/FLETCH use and deferred
  RLINE extraction.

## Later phases

- Consumer probes for BANISH, LUCIA, CANON, PORTO, CERES, and MAXIM.
- Publisher artifacts for CROP, PEBBLE, FLETCH, and PROOF.
- Extraction decision for any repeated calendar, obligation, or meaning-context
  primitives.
