# RITE Review Organization

RITE reviews protect ritual, taboo, sacred place, calendar, obligation, and
legitimacy records without turning RITE into theology, law, narrative
interpretation, economy, geography, or gameplay.

## Panel

| ID | Role | Lens |
|---|---|---|
| R-1 | Obligation Steward | Required actions, forbidden actions, offerings, and status changes |
| R-2 | Place/Calendar Keeper | Sacred places, timing, procession, mourning, and festival windows |
| R-3 | Meaning Boundary Skeptic | Keeps theology, law, and story interpretation out |
| R-4 | Consumer Adapter | BANISH, LUCIA, CANON, PORTO, CERES, MAXIM, and TRACKER usefulness |
| R-5 | Evidence Custodian | Source refs, anchors, and pack readiness |

## Review order

1. Use the panel for rite/obligation schema changes and consumer fixtures.
2. Use the Meaning Boundary Skeptic before accepting adjudication or doctrine.
3. Use the Evidence Custodian before publishing ritual/meaning packs.
